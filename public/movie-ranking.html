The Best TV series According to Illia

My TOP 3 TV series of all-time.

The breaking bad

Walter White, a chemistry teacher, discovers that he has cancer and decides
to get into the meth-making business to repay his medical debts.
His priorities begin to change when he partners with Jesse.

Game of Thrones

Nine noble families wage war against each other in order to gain control over the mythical land of Westeros.
Meanwhile, a force is rising after millenniums and threatens the existence of living men.

Stranger Things

In 1980s Indiana, a group of young friends witness supernatural forces and secret government exploits.
As they search for answers, the children unravel a series of extraordinary mysteries.
